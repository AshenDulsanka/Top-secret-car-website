How to Buy TOP SECRET Inventory Vehicles?

Importing your dream vehicle from other countries is not as difficult as you think!
Here are 7 easy steps of purchasing process of TOP SECRET Co. Ltd. inventory cars

  • 1) SELECT - Choose a vehicle from our inventory
  • 2) ORDER - Fill in the order sheet for the vehicle of your choice
    and wait for a confirmation e-mail or call from our staff
  • 3) INVOICE - An issued proforma invoice will be sent to you by e-mail
  • 4) PAYMENT - Transfer the total cost of the vehicle to TOP SECRET Co. Ltd.'s bank
  • 5) SHIPMENT - After your payment is confirmed, we will book the space
    on the soonest vessel headed towards your closest port. Both
    departure and arrival dates as well as other shipment details will be sent by e-mail
  • 6) PAPERWORK - Bill of landing, de-registration/export certificate of your car
    and a commercial invoice will be send to you by DHL after the vessel leaves our port
  • 7) DRIVE - Bring the documents to your custom agent and ENJOY THE DRIVE!!!

*If you would like to have any sort of assistance in regards with above specified procedures, please fell free to contact us!
